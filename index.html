Market & Standards

Why This Layer Matters Beyond Features

For legal, policy, and strategy stakeholders: standards compatibility, market implications, and the role of verifiable origin.
Different layer from C2PA

C2PA records how content was created.

Showplly defines what the product is.

This is not a replacement argument. It is a layer argument. Content provenance matters, but product identity must begin before content spreads.

Why this matters

Metadata alone does not establish product reality.

C2PA helps record declarations. Showplly helps establish product identity.

A product image can carry metadata and still lack any structural proof that the product itself is real, attributable, and continuously linked to its source.

Standards compatibility

Independent core. Compatible by design.

Future-proof flexibility without system dependency.

Showplly operates independently at the identity layer. Standards such as C2PA can be integrated when needed, without becoming the foundation of the system.

Regulation

Disclosure is not the same as verifiable origin.

Rules may require labels. Markets will require structure.

As standards evolve, platforms will increasingly be judged by whether the origin and continuity of products can be verified.
